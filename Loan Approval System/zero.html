What is a credit score?

  • A credit score is a numerical expression based on a level analysis of a person's credit files, to represent the credit worthiness of an individual.
  • It is based on a credit report information typically sourced from credit bureaus.
  • Lenders, such as banks use credit scores to evaluate the potential risk posed by lending money to consumers and to minimize losses due to bad debt.

CIBIL Score?

  • In India, there are four credit information companies licensed by Reserve Bank of India. The Credit Information Bureau (India) Limited (CIBIL) has functioned as a Credit Information Company from January 2001.
  • The CIBIL credit score is a three-digit number that represents a summary of individuals' credit history and credit rating. This score ranges from 300 to 900, with 900 being the best score.
  • It is the most popular credit score in India.
